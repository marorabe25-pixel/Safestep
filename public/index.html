How It Works

Prevention in four steps.

No behavioral change required. Just wear your shoes.

👟
Step 01
Slip it in.
Ultra-thin 3mm medical silicone. Activates automatically. Fits any shoe.
🌡️
Step 02
It reads the signals.
256 micro-sensors map pressure and temperature every 50ms — invisible to the human eye.
📡
Step 03
Data reaches your team.
Bluetooth 5.0 streams encrypted data to the SafeStep app and physician dashboard instantly.
🔔
Step 04
Alerts. Before damage.
AI flags anomalies 48–72 hours before symptoms. Patient and doctor notified instantly.
